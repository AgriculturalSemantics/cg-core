Map

Map
Class
Identifier http://purl.org/cg/terms/Map
Definition Any material representing the whole or part of the earth or any celestial body at any scale. Cartographic materials include two- and three-dimensional maps and plans (including maps of imaginary places), aeronautical, navigational, and celestial charts, atlases, globes, block diagrams, sections, aerial photographs with a cartographic purpose, bird's-eye views (map views), etc. primarily for human reading, not machine processing
Comments COAR
